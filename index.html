LORE

Before our droids, before our wars, before we truly the dominating species in the world , there was an ancient civilization that guarded an incredible power. A power so strong that it could easily change the course of history simply by misuse. Our ancestors, in fear of this power being abused, split it into 5 idols and stored each in different parts of the world. Though broken apart, the idols were said to have connections to the spiritual world, and even hosted powerful spirits themselves. These idols stayed hidden for centuries, but power and greed will eventually find them and ignite the great flame that will consume all...
